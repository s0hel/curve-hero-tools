Curve Dental features specialized AI agents designed to automate and streamline specific daily dental tasks natively on its platform. These skills are organized under the Curve+ AI suite and specialized partner integrations, taking care of front desk admin, clinical charting, and billing. [1, 2, 3, 4]  
Core Agent Skills 

• Clinical & Charting 

	• Notes+ (Ambient Scribe): Listens to patient encounters and automatically drafts provider-ready notes in SOAP format. 
	• Charting+ & Perio+ (Voice Commands): Allows providers and hygienists to update odontograms and dictate periodontal charting directly via natural speech. 
	• Radiographic AI (via Pearl): Acts as a clinical aid to automatically flag radiographic findings on bitewing and periapical X-rays. [5, 6, 7, 8]  

• Front Office & Patient Engagement 

	• AI Receptionist / Voice Agents: Automatically answers inbound patient calls, books or cancels appointments, and routes messages without tying up staff. 
	• Call Summarization: Transcribes and summarizes patient phone calls, automatically entering intelligence back into the patient's record. [8, 9, 10, 11]  

• Billing & Insurance 

	• Eligibility+ (Verification): Instantly checks and automates insurance eligibility and benefits verification in seconds instead of minutes. 
	• Revenue Intelligence (Collect+): Provides AI-powered capabilities to streamline collections, manage payments, and recover revenue. [1, 4, 12]  

• Software Training 

	• Ask CurveAI: Acts as a 24/7 personal software expert, offering step-by-step guidance, training, and self-help without needing to log a support ticket. [4]  

Platform Integration 
While Curve has native tools like Notes+ and Eligibility+, they also maintain strong, built-in partner integrations for specialized tasks. Popular voice and workflow add-ons include: 

• Bola AI: Used for restorative charting, perio charting, and clinical notations. 
• Kickcall: Specialized for AI voice receptionists synced directly to Curve. 
• HealOS: Adds AI agents for insurance authorization, prior authorizations, and faxes. [9, 10, 13]
